Amrita University

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multi-disciplinary, research-intensive, private university, educating a vibrant student population of over 30,000 by 2000+ strong faculty. Accredited with the highest possible A++ grade by NAAC, Amrita offers more than 300 UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs across various disciplines, including Engineering, Management, Medical Sciences, Ayurveda, Life Sciences, Physical Sciences, Agricultural Sciences, Law, Arts & Humanities, and Social & Behavioral Sciences.

SRM University AP

SRM University-AP, Andhra Pradesh is a private university in India. It was established in 2017 by the SRM Trust. The university is located in the city of Amaravati, the capital of Andhra Pradesh. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields such as engineering, management, science, and humanities.

VIT AP


VIT AP University

VIT-AP University is a private university located in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is part of the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) group of institutions. Established in 2017, VIT-AP offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields such as engineering, science, management, and humanities. The university aims to provide quality education and foster research and innovation among its students and faculty.
