Chennai Campus



The Chennai campus is located in Tamil Nadu. It mainly focuses on engineering and technology programs. The campus has modern classrooms. Laboratories are fully equipped with advanced systems. Students receive practical exposure to real-world projects. Technical workshops are conducted regularly. Seminars and conferences are organized. Cultural activities are encouraged. Sports facilities are available for students. The campus library provides digital resources. Faculty members are experienced and supportive. Placement training sessions are conducted. Internship opportunities are provided. Industry collaborations are maintained. Students are trained in communication skills. Entrepreneurship programs are available. Innovation projects are supported. Industrial visits are arranged. The campus maintains high academic standards. The environment is disciplined and student-friendly. The campus aims to produce skilled professionals.
