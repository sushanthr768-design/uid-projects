Amritapuri Campus



The Amritapuri campus is located in Kerala near the Arabian Sea. It is known for its peaceful and spiritual environment. The campus offers engineering, arts, and science programs. Students from different parts of India study here. The infrastructure is modern and well maintained. Classrooms are spacious and digitally equipped. Laboratories contain advanced equipment. The campus library has a vast collection of books. Research activities are strongly encouraged. Workshops and seminars are conducted regularly. Students participate in national and international conferences. Cultural festivals are celebrated every year. Sports facilities are available for students. Hostel facilities are comfortable and secure. Faculty members are highly experienced. Students receive career guidance and mentoring. Placement training programs are conducted. Industry collaborations are available. The campus promotes innovation and creativity. Community service activities are organized. The focus is on academic excellence and character development.
