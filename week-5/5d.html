Amaravati Campus



The Amaravati campus is located in Andhra Pradesh. It provides a peaceful academic atmosphere. The infrastructure is modern and well designed. Classrooms are equipped with smart boards. The campus offers undergraduate programs. Postgraduate programs are also available. Laboratories are fully equipped. The library contains thousands of books. Digital learning resources are accessible. Students participate in workshops. Technical events are conducted regularly. Cultural festivals are celebrated. Sports facilities are available. Hostel facilities are comfortable. Faculty members are experienced. Research activities are encouraged. Industrial visits are arranged. Placement training is provided. Students receive career guidance. Innovation and startup ideas are supported. The campus focuses on overall personality development.
